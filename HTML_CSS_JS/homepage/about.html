Our Practices

Plants aren't only nature's solar panels - they're also busy capturing CO2. We use biomass (harvested plants and such) to generate energy and transform that CO2 into something useful - bringing us nearer to a world where the more energy we produce, the more CO2 we remove from the atmosphere - better, right?

By using plants for electricity we can isolate the CO2 that was in the atmosphere. We're not only generating energy; we're actively removing greenhouse gasses.

There's more! We can also transform CO2 into fertilizer by reacting the combustion exhaust gas with ammonia. And guess what? We can even produce the ammonia from the gasified biomass. We're not only reversing our carbon emissions, we're also creating useful products which help grow more energy, and so, capture more carbon! It's like a ever-growing cycle of awesomeness.:!

We're creating a system that works with nature, not against it. It's a system that allows us to prioritize soil health, biodiversity, and reducing inputs like herbicides and pesticides. And these practices too help us remove CO2 from the atmosphere.

We also use solar-generated electricity to produce hydrogen from the electrolysis of water. So, not only are we harnessing the power of the sun, but we're also creating a clean, renewable source of energy. It's like we're living in the future!

Our Principles

Since our purpose is to remove as many greenhouse gasses as possible we could only benefit from helping you do the same. Therefore, our products, research, and inventions are now, and will always be Open Source.

Our goal is to remove as much CO2 from the atmosphere as possible. We strive do this by giving more than we get. We will give more energy than we use, we will give more money than we keep, and we will give more time than we need.

We welcome every opportunity to collaborate, assist, and support those who wish to reach the same goals. If you would like to collaborate on your idea of making the world a better place, we will share our resources, or even create a way to make it reality - we will always be better together.

We will be sustainable in our practices, our finances, and our goals.
